# NUR Privacy Statement  
Effective Date: January 7, 2026


## 1. Data Collection
To provide accurate thermal analysis and wellness insights, we collect the following information:


Identity & Health Profile:  
Name, email address, date of birth, biological sex, height, and weight.


Clinical Indicators:  
Self-reported chronic conditions (e.g., Diabetes, Arthritis), activity levels, and recent lifestyle factors (e.g., caffeine intake, physiotherapy, or sun exposure).


Symptom Mapping:  
User-reported pain locations, intensity, and duration submitted through our digital body map.


---


## 2. Image Processing & Purging Policy
We prioritize your physical privacy through a “Processing-Only” visual data model.


Instance Processing:  
Our system captures standard visual images solely to conduct body-contour mapping and ensure thermal data is correctly aligned with your anatomy.


Automatic Purging:  
Once the analysis instance is complete and your thermal health results are generated, all standard visual images are immediately and permanently purged.


Non-Accessibility:  
These visual images are never stored in your account, are not accessible to Amplifai Health staff, and cannot be retrieved once the session ends.


---


## 3. Data Sharing
Amplifai Health does not sell your personal health data to third-party advertisers.


Personal data may only be shared with trusted service providers when necessary to operate the NUR platform and deliver the requested services. These providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the purposes specified by Amplifai Health.


---


## 4. Wearable Data Integration (Optional)
You may choose to connect a compatible wearable device to the NUR platform to enhance the wellness insights provided by the system.


If enabled, we may collect certain wellness metrics made available through your wearable provider. These may include, depending on the device and permissions granted:


- Heart rate and heart rate variability  
- Activity metrics such as steps, movement, and exercise duration  
- Sleep metrics  
- Stress or recovery indicators  
- Other wellness-related metrics available through your wearable provider


Wearable data is obtained through secure integrations with authorized third-party wearable platforms and APIs.


The availability and accuracy of this data depends on the wearable device manufacturer and its associated services.


---


## 5. Third-Party Wearable Providers
When you connect a wearable device, you authorize Amplifai Health to retrieve wearable data through approved third-party platforms and APIs required to facilitate the integration.


Please note:


- Wearable data may pass through third-party infrastructure necessary to enable the integration.  
- Amplifai Health does not control how wearable device manufacturers collect, store, or process data outside the NUR platform.  
- Your use of wearable devices remains subject to the privacy policies and terms of the respective wearable providers.


Amplifai Health does not sell wearable data to advertisers.


---


## 6. Wearable Data Usage
Wearable data collected through integrations is used solely to:


- Enhance wellness insights generated by NUR  
- Correlate physiological signals with thermal analysis results  
- Generate wellness indicators and recovery insights within the NUR platform


Wearable data is not used for advertising, marketing profiling, or resale.
