Overview

Classification Framework

The athlete performance software market spans six distinct functional categories. Most platforms operate within a single category with varying depth. A smaller number of integrated platforms aggregate across categories. NUR Platform sits within Category 06 — Integrated Multi-Modal — as its sole occupant to fuse thermal imaging with physiological readiness into a deterministic clinical decision support framework. No other platform in this category integrates thermographic tissue stress as a first-class computational input.

CAT 05

Thermal & Imaging

Platforms using non-contact or imaging-based modalities to detect tissue-level inflammation, circulatory changes, or structural stress. Thermography measures contralateral skin-surface temperature asymmetry as a proxy for underlying tissue stress.

Provider Products What It Measures / Does Target Audience
ThermoHuman ThermoHuman Platform Sports thermography platform — athlete thermal mapping, population-norm comparisons, and longitudinal asymmetry tracking. Closest existing commercial analogue to the thermal component of NUR/COMPASS. Thermal-only; does not fuse with systemic physiological readiness. Physiotherapists, sports scientists
Meditherm Digitherm Clinical thermography analysis with built-in normative databases and region-of-interest analysis. Used in sports medicine and oncology screening. Sports medicine physicians, clinical practitioners
Myovision ScanVision Surface EMG combined with paraspinal thermal scanning. Used in chiropractic and sports rehabilitation to detect bilateral neuromuscular asymmetries. Chiropractors, sports rehabilitation clinicians

NUR Platform — COMPASS NUR + COMPASS Framework · Amplifai Health Fuses thermographic tissue stress (Thermal Asymmetry Index) with systemic physiological readiness (HRV, sleep, ACWR) into a unified 0–100 COMPASS score with deterministic, zone-based classification. Sole platform to integrate thermal imaging as a first-class computational input. Explicitly detects Deceptive Readiness — the clinically high-risk state where wearable data appears normal while local tissue is inflamed. Beyond scoring, NURAI generates a personalised intervention plan from the full clinical dataset — thermal asymmetry per body region, physiological metrics, reported pain entries, and athlete demographics — producing phased rehabilitation guidance and reassessment triggers tailored to each individual, not just a generic zone response. Physiotherapists, coaches, athletes

All other Category 06 platforms aggregate GPS load, HRV, and wellness data — but none integrate thermographic tissue stress as a first-class computational input, and none produce intervention plans from the full clinical dataset. NUR Platform is the only integrated platform with a thermal modality and the only one that synthesises thermal asymmetry, physiological metrics, pain entries, and athlete demographics into a personalised, phased intervention plan.

The Structural Moat

The Deceptive Readiness zone — where an athlete's wearable data appears normal while local tissue is inflamed — is invisible to every Tier 1 competitor. Detecting it requires both axes present in the same scoring model. This is the condition most likely to result in soft-tissue injury under full training load, and it is the highest-value clinical insight NUR produces.
